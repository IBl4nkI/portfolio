About Me

I love hearing feedback from others in order for my project to be the best that I can make it, so when someone suggests something that will make my project better, even if it is insanely difficult to implement, I will find a way.

As an aspiring software developer, I have already created many projects that I am incredibly proud of, but I am still eager to keep creating more and learn things as I go. I am also aspiring to be a professional video editor for films, social media content, and also my own personal projects.
